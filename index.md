<h2>Research</h2>
<p>My primary interest lies in the theoretical and practical aspects of learning and control for various types of linear and nonlinear dynamical systems. I focus on data-driven and learning-based methods for modeling, optimization, model reduction, and control of dynamical systems.</p>
